Whitepaper

Snake NFT is a Play-to-Earn project built on the WAX blockchain. Players can stake their Snake NFTs of different rarities to generate daily WAX rewards. Each rarity tier provides a unique reward rate, designed to ensure fairness and long-term sustainability of the ecosystem.

Our mission is to create a fun, rewarding, and community-driven economy where Snake NFT holders benefit from both gameplay and token rewards. The project will expand over time with new game mechanics, seasonal events, and collaborations across the WAX ecosystem. Transparency and fair distribution are our core principles. By holding and staking your Snake NFTs, you become part of the growing Snake Metaverse.

Roadmap

  • Q1 2025: Launch of Snake NFT Pack and basic staking system.
  • Q2 2025: Integration of in-app shop and marketplace features.
  • Q3 2025: Competitive player events with boosted rewards.
  • Q4 2025: Expansion of the ecosystem and partnerships with other WAX projects.
  • 2026 and beyond: New gameplay features, seasonal packs, and community-driven governance.
